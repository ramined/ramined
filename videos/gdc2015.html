GDC: Rebooting a 4 Year Old Portfolio to the Top of the Charts

Company : Halfbrick
Date : March 2015
Role : Chief Product Officer

I gave this talk at GDC 2014. It covers the efforts we took to turn around an aging portfolio, and how we growth hacked our way back to the top of the App Store. I explain the principles behind Games as a Service, and the technical and process changes required to grow a title to the top.

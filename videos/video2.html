Finnish Parliament: Fostering Entrepreneurship

Date : September 2010
Role : Head Coach at Summer of Startups

This is an extract from the talk I gave at the Finnish Internet Forum. The event was organized by the Finnish Parliament, to discuss issues such as the future of the Internet and of technology entrepreneurship. I discussed the challenges of cloud computing and privacy laws (due to my experience with MySites), and my experience co-founding a program called Summer of Startups.

GDC - From F2P to Indie: Walking a Mile in an Indie's Shoes

Date : March 2016

I helped with Yahtzee Croshaw (of Zero Punctuation fame), as the producer for the game The Consuming Shadow. I explain how we climbed Steam Greenlight, approached the game's distribution, and the challenges for game developers. This talk is an early example of explaining the discovery problem on a platform such as Steam.
